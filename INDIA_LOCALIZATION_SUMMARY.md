## 📄 Content Guidelines (For Future Updates)

### Tone of Voice
- **Professional** but **approachable**
- **Confident** without being **arrogant**
- **Transparent** about **process and pricing**
- **Empathetic** to **customer concerns**

### Key Messages
1. No hidden costs or surprise bills
2. Complete transparency through digital portal
3. RERA compliance and legal protection
4. One-stop solution from approval to handover
5. Quality materials from trusted brands

### Avoid
- Over-promising timelines
- Vague pricing ("contact for quote")
- Technical jargon without explanation
- Comparison with competitors
- Negative framing
